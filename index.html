Women Helpline

1091

Toll-free helpline for women facing violence, abuse, or any distress. Dial anytime, anywhere in India.

Emergency

Emergency Services

112

India's unified emergency response number for police, fire & ambulance. Works even without a SIM card.

Report Online

Cyber Crime Helpline

1930

Report online harassment, stalking, morphing, or any cyber crime against women. Also visit cybercrime.gov.in.

Safety Tip: Save these numbers on speed-dial. Share them with every woman you know. In an emergency, press the power button 5 times quickly on most smartphones to trigger an SOS.
